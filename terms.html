Understanding our restoration process and services.

Restoration Services

TruePortrait Revival provides artistic photograph restoration services designed to enhance, preserve, and honor treasured memories.

Restoration Results

Every photograph is unique. Results depend on the original quality, age, damage, and available details within the photograph.

Family Legacy Portraits

Family Legacy Portraits are meaningful artistic compositions created from individual photographs to honor relationships and preserve family connections.

These creations represent a tribute to family history and are not intended to represent an original photograph of an event that occurred.

Client Responsibility

Clients are responsible for ensuring they have permission or ownership rights to photographs submitted for restoration.
